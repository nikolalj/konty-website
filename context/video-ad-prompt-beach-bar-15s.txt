SHOT-BY-SHOT TIMELINE

SHOT 1 (00:00–00:02) — Paper Chaos
Handheld camera shake, shallow depth-of-field. Extreme close-up of a hand scribbling on a crumpled paper ledger on a beach bar counter. Wind catches the page corner. Harsh midday Adriatic sun, overexposed. Unstable energy. Exits with whip pan right carrying motion blur into Shot 2.

SHOT 2 (00:02–00:04) — Problem Montage
Speed ramp acceleration + jump cuts + desaturation. Three rapid cuts in 2 seconds: (a) wide shot of half-occupied sunbeds, confused staff holding receipt book, (b) close-up of cash slipped into waist apron instead of register, (c) empty sunbed with crumpled towel, guest gone, no record. Handheld, desaturated, each cut faster than the last.

SHOT 3 (00:04–00:05) — White Flash Pivot
Full-frame white bloom flash, 0.4 seconds. Faint sunbed row silhouettes visible through the overexposure. This is the narrative pivot — everything after is clean, warm, controlled.

SHOT 4 (00:05–00:08) — Digital Beach Floor Plan (SIGNATURE SHOT)
Digital zoom scale-out + slow-motion at 70% + warm colour shift. Starts tight on a tablet showing Konty POS beach floor plan with coloured zones (blue VIP, green standard, amber economy) and status badges. Pulls back over 2 seconds to reveal a confident beach manager at the bar counter, turquoise Adriatic Sea behind them. Saturation increases, golden hour tones bloom. Smooth, controlled, expansive.

SHOT 5 (00:08–00:10) — QR Ordering Split
Diagonal split-screen composite at 30°. Left: tourist hand scanning QR code on parasol with phone. Right: order appearing on POS screen in bar. Speed ramp decelerates from fast scan to normal. Golden light on beach side, cooler interior on POS side. Static composition.

SHOT 6 (00:10–00:12) — Aerial Beach Wide
Slow ascending drone shot with gentle scale-out. Montenegrin beach from above — organized parasol and sunbed rows, turquoise water, coastal mountains. Speed at 80%. Peak golden hour warmth. The organized rows echo the digital floor plan from Shot 4.

SHOT 7 (00:12–00:15) — Logo Resolve
0.5s dissolve from aerial into warm gradient background (sunset amber to sky blue). Konty logo center-frame, white, subtle scale-in 98%→100%. Faint light dust particles drift across frame. Hold 1.5 seconds.
