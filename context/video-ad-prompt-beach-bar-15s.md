# Konty Beach Bar — 15s Video Ad Prompt

**Target:** Montenegro beach bar operators (B2B). Meta/Instagram Reels, 9:16 vertical.
**Landing page:** konty.com/me/solutions/beach-bar

---

## Prompt

A 15-second cinematic ad on a sunny Adriatic beach in Montenegro. Opens with a close-up of a hand frantically scribbling on a crumpled paper ledger at a beach bar counter, harsh midday sun, handheld camera, slightly shaky and tense. Quick cut to a confused staff member standing among half-occupied sunbeds holding a receipt book, then a close-up of cash being slipped into a waist apron. Desaturated, uneasy mood. A bright white flash fills the frame — the turning point. The mood shifts completely. A confident beach manager holds a tablet showing a colorful digital beach floor plan with blue, green, and amber sunbed zones. The camera slowly pulls back to reveal the turquoise Adriatic Sea behind them. Golden hour light, warm saturated colors, smooth controlled camera movement. A tourist scans a QR code on a parasol with their phone while the order appears on a screen at the bar. Wide aerial drone shot ascending over a Montenegrin beach — organized rows of parasols and sunbeds, turquoise water, coastal mountains, golden light. Gently dissolves into a warm sunset-to-sky gradient. White Konty logo appears center frame. Clean, aspirational, resolved.

---

## Production Notes

- **Narrative arc:** Chaos (paper, theft, confusion) → white flash pivot → Solution (digital control, QR ordering, organized beach) → Brand resolve
- **Color grade:** Desaturated and tense for the first 5 seconds, then shifts to warm golden hour for the remaining 10 seconds
- **Camera:** Handheld/shaky in the problem section, smooth/controlled in the solution section, drone for the aerial
- **Text overlays to add in post:** "Papir, olovka i haos?" (0-2s), "Vaša plaža. Pod vašom kontrolom." (7s), Konty logo + konty.com + "Pišite nam na Viber" (12-15s)
- **Audio direction:** Tense ambient sounds for the opening, clean chime on the white flash transition, warm uplifting pad for the solution section, resolving to silence on the brand card
