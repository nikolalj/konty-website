# Konty Beach Bar — 15s Video Ad Prompt

**Target:** Montenegro beach bar operators (B2B). Meta/Instagram Reels, 9:16 vertical.
**Narrative:** Pain (paper chaos, no control) → Transition → Solution (Konty digital beach management)
**Mood arc:** Frustration/chaos → clarity/relief → confidence/warmth
**Text overlays in Montenegrin.**
**Landing page:** konty.com/me/solutions/beach-bar

---

## SHOT-BY-SHOT EFFECTS TIMELINE

### SHOT 1 (00:00-00:02) — Paper Chaos Close-Up

- EFFECT: Handheld camera shake + shallow depth-of-field
- Extreme close-up of a hand scribbling on a crumpled, sun-bleached paper ledger on a beach bar counter. Wind catches the corner of the page.
- Warm, overexposed natural light — harsh midday Adriatic sun
- Shallow focus isolates the pen on paper, background is blown-out beach blur
- Camera has slight handheld drift — unstable, uneasy energy
- Speed: real-time, no ramp
- TEXT OVERLAY fades in at 00:01: **"Papir, olovka i haos?"** (white text, subtle drop shadow)
- Exits with a quick whip pan RIGHT carrying motion blur into Shot 2

### SHOT 2 (00:02-00:04) — The Problem Montage

- EFFECT: Speed ramp (acceleration) + jump cuts + desaturation shift
- Three rapid jump cuts compressed into 2 seconds:
  - (a) Wide shot: row of sunbeds, half-occupied, a confused staff member looks around holding a receipt book — who's paid, who hasn't?
  - (b) Close-up: cash being pocketed — a hand slides a banknote into a waist apron instead of the register
  - (c) Medium shot: empty sunbed with a crumpled towel — the guest left, no record exists
- Each sub-frame is approximately 0.6s with hard cuts between them
- Colour grade shifts slightly desaturated — muted warmth, tension
- Speed accelerates through the three cuts (each slightly faster than the last)
- Camera: handheld, slightly low angle for (a), tight for (b), static for (c)
- TEXT OVERLAY at 00:03: **"Znate li sta se desava na vasoj plazi?"** — text appears with a subtle scale-in (105% → 100%)

### SHOT 3 (00:04-00:05) — The Turn / White Flash

- EFFECT: White bloom flash (full frame) + audio beat sync
- Full-frame white flash — overexposed bloom lasting approximately 0.4s
- Functions as the emotional pivot point: chaos ends, solution begins
- During the flash, faint silhouette outlines of sunbed rows are barely visible through the bloom
- Audio: a clean, resonant "tap" or digital chime sound marks the transition
- This is the NARRATIVE PIVOT — everything after this is clean, warm, controlled

### SHOT 4 (00:05-00:08) — The Digital Beach Floor Plan (Signature Shot)

- EFFECT: Digital zoom (scale-out pull-back) + slow-motion + warm colour grade shift
- Camera starts tight on a tablet screen showing the Konty POS beach floor plan — coloured sunbed zones (blue VIP row, green standard, amber economy), status badges visible
- Slow digital zoom pulls back to reveal the tablet being held by a confident beach manager standing at a beach bar counter, turquoise Adriatic Sea visible behind them
- The pull-back takes approximately 2 seconds — controlled, smooth, expansive
- Colour grade shifts warm: saturation increases, golden hour tones bloom in
- Speed: approximately 70% (subtle slow-motion on the pull-back for cinematic weight)
- The manager's expression is calm, assured — contrast to the confused staff in Shot 2
- This is the SIGNATURE VISUAL EFFECT — the moment the viewer sees what the product actually looks like in context
- TEXT OVERLAY at 00:07: **"Vasa plaza. Pod vasom kontrolom."** — text fades in with letter-by-letter stagger (left to right), white text with warm shadow

### SHOT 5 (00:08-00:10) — QR Ordering from Sunbed

- EFFECT: Split-screen composite (diagonal) + speed ramp (deceleration)
- Diagonal split composition: left half shows a tourist's hand scanning a QR code on a parasol with their phone, right half shows the order appearing on the POS screen in the bar
- The split is a clean diagonal line at approximately 30 degrees — modern, dynamic
- Both halves play simultaneously — cause and effect in one frame
- Speed ramp: starts fast (the scan) then decelerates to normal speed (the order appearing)
- Warm, saturated colour — golden afternoon light on the beach side, cooler interior light on the POS side
- Camera: static composition, the movement comes from the action within the split

### SHOT 6 (00:10-00:12) — Aerial Beach Wide

- EFFECT: Slow ascending drone shot + gentle digital zoom (scale-out)
- Wide aerial/drone-perspective shot of a Montenegrin beach — rows of parasols and sunbeds organized in clean lines, turquoise water, dramatic coastal mountains in background
- Camera slowly ascends and pulls back — revealing the full scope of the beach
- Speed: approximately 80% (slightly slowed for grandeur)
- Colour grade: peak warmth — golden hour, saturated blues and ambers
- The organized rows of sunbeds visually echo the digital floor plan from Shot 4 — the connection is implicit
- No text overlay — let the visual breathe

### SHOT 7 (00:12-00:15) — Logo Resolve + CTA

- EFFECT: Fade transition (dissolve, 0.5s) + subtle particle/light dust
- The aerial shot gently dissolves into a warm gradient background (sunset amber to soft sky blue — matches the konty.com/me/solutions/beach-bar hero gradient)
- Konty logo appears center-frame — clean, white, no animation beyond a subtle scale-in (98% → 100% over 0.5s)
- Faint light dust particles drift slowly across the frame — gives texture and warmth to the brand card
- TEXT BELOW LOGO at 00:13: **"konty.com"** — clean, small, white
- TEXT AT BOTTOM at 00:14: **"Pisite nam na Viber →"** — CTA text, slightly smaller
- Hold for 1.5s — let the brand register
- Audio: gentle resolution tone, warm pad, fade to silence

   — Full-frame overexposed flash as the narrative pivot
   — The most emotionally charged single frame in the edit

3. **DIGITAL ZOOM / SCALE** (used 2x)
   — Shot 4 (pull-back reveal), 6 (aerial scale-out)
   — Controlled pull-backs create an "opening up" feeling — from problem to possibility

4. **SLOW-MOTION** (used 2x)
   — Shots 4 (subtle 70%), 6 (subtle 80%)
   — Gentle slow-motion in the solution act adds weight and confidence

   — Shots 1, 2
   — Confined to the "problem" act only — instability = chaos
   — Completely absent from the "solution" act — stability = control

6. **JUMP CUTS** (used 1x)
   — Shot 2
   — Three rapid cuts compressing the problem montage

7. **SPLIT-SCREEN COMPOSITE** (used 1x)
   — Shot 5

8. **WHIP PAN TRANSITION** (used 1x)

9. **DESATURATION SHIFT** (used 1x)

## ENERGY ARC

**Act 1 — CHAOS (00:00-00:05):** Maximum instability. Handheld camera, harsh midday light, desaturated tones, rapid jump cuts. The viewer feels the frustration of paper-based beach management — the confusion, the theft, the lack of control. The white bloom flash at 00:04 is the breaking point: enough.

**Act 2 — CLARITY (00:05-00:10):** The energy inverts. Camera becomes controlled and smooth. Colour saturates into warm golden tones. The Konty POS floor plan is revealed on a tablet with a confident pull-back — the signature shot. The split-screen QR ordering shot shows the product in action without needing explanation. Every visual choice signals: this is organized, this is modern, this is under control.

**Act 3 — CONFIDENCE (00:10-00:15):** Energy resolves completely. A majestic aerial of an organized Montenegrin beach — the visual payoff of what digital management enables. Dissolves to the warm Konty brand card. The tagline "Vasa plaza. Pod vasom kontrolom." has already landed in Shot 4 — the logo card just seals it. The viewer is left with warmth, aspiration, and a clear next step.
